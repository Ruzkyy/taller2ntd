Adoption Guidelines

Adopting a pet is a great responsibility. Please read our guidelines before applying:

  • Age Requirement: The adopter must be at least 18 years old.
  • Responsible Ownership: The adopter must ensure a safe and loving environment for the pet.
  • Home Visit: In some cases, a home visit may be required before finalizing the adoption.
  • Sterilization and Vaccination: All pets are sterilized, vaccinated, and dewormed before adoption.
  • No Resale or Abandonment: The adopter agrees not to resell or abandon the pet under any circumstances.
  • Adoption Fee: A small fee may apply to cover veterinary expenses.
  • Commitment: The adopter must be willing to provide lifelong care, including food, medical attention, and affection.

By adopting, you are giving a pet a second chance at a loving home. Thank you for choosing adoption!
